# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Collaborators, clients, and developers looking to understand Thalari Koushik's skillset, personal brand, and engineering craft.

## Product Purpose
A high-impact personal branding and portfolio website demonstrating state-of-the-art engineering, motion design, and product building capabilities.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, bold, premium, Swiss/modern grotesque, high-contrast.

## Anti-references
Generic template landing pages, low-contrast gray text, SaaS-cliché grids, and sluggish transitions.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity and Intention**: Every element exists for a functional or clear visual reason.
- **Extreme Contrast**: Pure black (#000000) and crisp white (#ffffff) to build a premium editorial tone.
- **Physical Feel**: Scroll and click interactions must feel snappy and responsive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA contrast ratios, readable line lengths, and prefers-reduced-motion support.
